PRECISION-RECALL CURVE (DECISION TREE)

The graph below shows the precision-recall curve or PRC of decision tree algorithm with and without Adasyn-Enn. The three lines indicates the PRC of each classes and the average of the two. The solid black line corresponds to the class 0 which is the classes that don't have PCOS, the solid green line corresponds to the class 1, those that have PCOS and the dotted blue line is the average. While the PRC of class 0 is higher when there is no Adasyn-Enn, the PRC of class 1 is significantly higher if the Adasyn-Enn resulting to higher average PRC.
